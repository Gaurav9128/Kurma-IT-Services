about Me

Kurma IT Services' mission and passion.

welcome to our website

At Kurma IT Services, we are passionate about helping businesses achieve their full potential through the implementation and optimization of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions. With a track record of success and a team of dedicated experts, we have been elevating efficiency and empowering growth for organizations of all sizes and industries. As a leading provider of ERP consulting services, we specialize in the seamless integration of Infor ERP solutions. Our expertise lies in implementing and optimizing Infor LN, Infor CloudSuite, and other cutting-edge Infor software solutions. Through our extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of Infor ERP, we enable businesses to unlock their true potential and gain a competitive edge in today's rapidly evolving marketplace.

our services

ERP Implementation


We guide you through the process of selecting, customizing, and implementing the right ERP solution for your business.

ERP Implementation

System Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive assessment of your current processes and systems to determine the best Infor ERP solution for your business.

Customization and Configuration

Our team will customize and configure Infor ERP to match your unique workflows and business requirements.

Data Migration

Safely and efficiently migrate your existing data to the new Infor ERP system to ensure a smooth transition.

Testing and Quality Assurance

Rigorous testing and quality assurance procedures are in place to guarantee that your ERP system is fully functional and error-free.

Training and Support

We provide hands-on training to your team, ensuring they are fully equipped to leverage the Infor ERP system. Our support continues long after the implementation to address any questions or issues that may arise.

ERP Optimization


Already using an ERP system? We can fine-tune it for maximum efficiency and performance.

Training And Support


Our support doesn't end with implementation. We provide training and ongoing support to ensure you get the most from your ERP system.

Development And Integrations


We enhance and integrate Infor ERP systems with custom modules, workflow automation, UI enhancements, advanced reporting, and mobile solutions. We ensure seamless third-party and legacy system integration, unlocking your ERP's full potential for innovation, efficiency, and growth.
